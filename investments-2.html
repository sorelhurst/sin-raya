Investments

We invest in Small and Medium-Sized Enterprises with growth ambitions.

We like companies with a clear sense of purpose and their place in the universe. We like ambition and high-conviction growth stories. We like committed owners with a clear strategic vision that has withstood the test of time. We like a clear link between our involvement and unlocking the next stage of growth.

 

We offer more than just finance, and we look at more than just the numbers.

We like good margins and a clear ability to defend them. We like having a proven, experienced management team with a clear track record that demonstrates they can succeed in this next stage of growth. And once we invest, we are happy to make our world-class advisory and first-class network of partners and experts available to help realize their ambitions. 

 

We are global investors.

We are happy to consider any opportunity where we think we can make a difference. But we focus firmly in generating deal flow where we believe there are more and better opportunities... bridging the gap between the need to grow the next generation of economic champions and the financing constraints in place.
